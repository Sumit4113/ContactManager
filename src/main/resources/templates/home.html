About Contact Manager

Revolutionizing Contact Management

Contact Manager is a cutting-edge solution designed to streamline your contact management experience. WThis project is a Smart Contact Management System developed to help users store, organize, and manage their contacts efficiently.

It provides a secure and user-friendly platform where users can easily manage personal and professional contact information.

  • Secure password recovery using email verification
  • Store profile and contact images safely in the cloud
  • Add, view, update, and delete contacts easily
  • Secure password update option
  • Mobile-responsive design for on-the-go access
